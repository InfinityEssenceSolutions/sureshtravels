What are the requirements to rent a car?

To rent a car, you typically need a valid driver's license, a credit card, and to meet the age requirements set by the rental company, which can vary by location and car category.

What is included in the rental fee?

The rental fee usually includes basic insurance coverage, unlimited mileage, and taxes. Additional services like GPS, child seats, and full insurance coverage may come at an extra cost.

Can I return the rental car to a different location?

Yes, most car rental companies offer one-way rentals, allowing you to drop off the car at a different location. Additional fees may apply, so check with your rental provider.
